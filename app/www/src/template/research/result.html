Who We Are

Jobayan.com is a product of Openovate Labs, a full stack technology company that develops apps, companies and game changers.


We made Jobayan.com as an alternative channel for technology based companies, startups, and overall, non-corporate companies like our own, to simply and quickly find fresh and savvy talent. We took a deep look on how our sister companies Galleon and Aces Call Center Jobs acquire talent as well as other similar industries, tech, and startups alike. We have been collecting data on the hiring process for over a year and wanted to translate our findings through this website.


To our brothers and sisters at Startup PH (Philippines) community and the PHackers (Philippines) community, this one is for you.

The Jobayan's Mission

Job and talent matching without the hassle. Other job boards, are very corporate; its process is corporate; its timelines is corporate; its revenue is corporate; and its results are well … corporate. Jobayan.com is a simple form, auto matching and removes the awkwardness in networking.
