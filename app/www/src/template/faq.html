Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know all about Jobayan

Jobayan is a product of Openovate Labs, a full stack technology company that develops apps, companies and game changers. It is made initially as an alternative channel for technology-based companies, startups, and non-corporate companies like ours to quickly find fresh and savvy talent easily. We aim to shape Jobayan not as another job search engine. By allowing job seekers to promote their preferred job roles in the platform, it becomes a job community where getting hired and finding candidates are easy. Recruiters and job seekers match and connect with each other to make job hunting more social. Since operationalizing it on September 2017, almost 20,000 potential job seekers have signed up and 14,000 companies providing job opportunities in the platform.

Getting started in finding jobs

How do I sign up?

Click the button in the upper right corner of the site.
Enter your Name, Email, Password, click the captcha button and click or sign up via Facebook or Linkedin.

How do I search for a job?

In the search bar, you may enter the job position, location, or company you are looking for and press enter or click the the search icon.
*You don’t have to be logged in to view the search results.

How do I search for a company/recruiter?

In the search bar, you may enter the name of the company or recruiter and location you are looking for and press enter or click the the search icon.
*You don’t have to be logged in to view the search results.

How do I show interest in a job posting?

To show interest in a job post, click the {{_ 'Interested'}} button at the bottom right of every job posting. The company/recruiter will receive an email where your contact details are provided.
*You have to be logged in to show interest in a job posting. To log in, click the login button at the upper right corner of the site or simply click the {{_ 'Interested'}} button and it will redirect you to the log in page.

How do I post as a job seeker?

Click button.
Enter your Name, the Position you want to apply for, and in what Location. Click button.

How do I know if a company/recruiter is interested in me?

When a company/recruiter is interested in you, you will receive an email or an SMS.
You can reach out to them through the contact details provided.
PRO TIP: We recommend you include for full name, contact details, and resume when replying via email to a company that is interested in you.

Do I need to upload my resume?

Uploading a resume is optional but it will help get you a better chance to get matched.
Resume can only be viewed and downloaded by verified companies.

The position I am looking for is not in the Jobayan list. Can I still post it?

Definitely! The positions in our dropdown list serve as guide.

Getting started in finding employees

How do I sign up?

Click the button in the upper right corner of the site.
Enter your Name, Email, Password, click the captcha button and click or sign up via Facebook or Linkedin.
PRO TIP: Make Sure you click on the EMPLOYER tab under Create Account in the Sign up page.

How do I search for a Job Seeker?

In the search bar, you may enter the name of the of the position, field or name of the job seeker and location you are looking for and press enter or click the the search icon.
*You don’t have to be logged in to view the search results.

How do I show interest in a Job Seeker?

To show interest in a job seeker, click the {{_ 'Interested'}} button at the bottom right of every job posting. The job seeker will receive an email where your contact details are provided.
*You have to be logged in to show interest in a job posting. To log in, click the login button at the upper right corner of the site or simply click the {{_ 'Interested'}} button and it will redirect you to the log in page.

How do I know if a job seeker is interested in me/us?

There are many ways for you to be notified:
Via email - You will receive an email where the contact details of the interested job seeker are provided.
Via SMS - For only 500 credits per post, you don’t need to go online to check interested candidates. You can now receive notifications via SMS for a month from each job seekers that are interested on your job post.

How do I know if my job posting matched with a job seeker’s post?

There are many ways for you to be notified:
Via email - You will receive an email containing the job postings that matched with your post.
Via SMS - For only 300 credits per post, you can now receive match notifications via SMS for a month anytime, anywhere without the need to click interested button on job seekers.

How do I buy credits?

You may buy credits by logging in to your account. An button will appear at the upper right hand corner near your profile icon when you hover your cursor to your credit amount.
You will be redirected to the Buy Credits page where you can enter your credit card details and amount you want to purchase.

How do I use my credits?

You can use your credits to promote a post, download resumes, enable paid features such as SMS notification and many more!
Visit our Services and Pricing page for more details.

How do I promote a post?

You can promote your post by either searching for your post in the listings or choose the post you want to promote in your posts dashboard and click Promote button. You can access your posts dashboard by clicking onto your profile icon at the upper right hand corner and choosing Posts from the dropdown list.
You need to have 500 credits to promote a post.

How do I download a job seeker’s resume?

You can download a job seeker’s resume by clicking the {{_ 'Resume'}} button of the job seeker who is interested in you.
You may view the list of job seekers who are interested in you by clicking {{_ 'Interested'}} in the dropdown list when you click your profile in the upper right corner.

How do I get a verified badge?

To have your employer account reviewed for a verified badge, you may send an email to business@jobayan.com .
Our business team will review the accounts eligibility in order to avoid fraudulent accounts. May require additional documents if deemed necessary.

How do I avail the featured email campaign?

To request for your own featured email campaign, you may send an email to business@jobayan.com . Our business team would be happy to tailor fit a service for your recruitment needs.
